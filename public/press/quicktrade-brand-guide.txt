QuickTrade Brand Guide

Company
-------
QuickTrade is a New Zealand-built marketplace that helps homeowners and businesses connect with trusted local tradespeople.

QuickTrade is a New Zealand marketplace for local services and skilled trades. Built in Blenheim, Marlborough, the platform helps people post jobs, compare quotes, review worker profiles, and hire with more confidence.

QuickTrade was founded in Blenheim, New Zealand, with a focus on solving a practical Kiwi problem: finding reliable tradies quickly, safely, and without guesswork.

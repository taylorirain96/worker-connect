# Spring Home Maintenance Checklist NZ: 12 Jobs to Book Now

Spring is the perfect time to tackle home maintenance in New Zealand. After a winter of rain, wind, and cold, your property needs a check-up — and the warmer months are when tradies are at their most available (before the summer rush). Here's your complete spring home maintenance checklist for NZ homeowners.

## Why Spring Is the Best Time for Home Maintenance

- **Weather improves** — Ideal conditions for outdoor work like painting, roofing, and decking
- **Tradie availability** — Slightly easier to get quotes before the busy December–February period
- **Spot winter damage early** — Address leaks, rot, and condensation before they get worse
- **Prepare for summer** — Air conditioning, outdoor areas, and decks need to be ready

## The Spring Home Maintenance Checklist

### 1. ✅ Roof Inspection

Winter is hard on NZ roofs. Check for:
- Missing or cracked iron sheets / tiles
- Rust on metal roofing
- Damaged flashing around chimneys and skylights
- Blocked box gutters

A licensed [roofing contractor](/services/roofing) should inspect if you see any signs of water staining on ceilings.

### 2. ✅ Gutter Cleaning

Blocked gutters lead to overflow, water ingress, and foundation damage. After autumn leaves and winter debris, spring is the right time to have them cleared. A handyman or roofer can do this in 1–2 hours for most houses.

### 3. ✅ Heat Pump Service

Your heat pump has been working hard all winter. Book a service to:
- Clean filters
- Check refrigerant levels
- Inspect electrical connections
- Test cooling mode before summer hits

Most [heat pump technicians](/services/heat-pumps-air-conditioning) recommend an annual service. Spring is the ideal window.

### 4. ✅ Exterior Paint Check

Spring's dry weather is ideal for painting. Check exterior paintwork for:
- Peeling or flaking areas (especially on weatherboards)
- Mould or mildew growth (common in NZ's wet winters)
- Exposed timber that needs re-coating

A [local painter](/services/painting) can assess what needs doing and quote for touch-ups or a full repaint.

### 5. ✅ Deck and Outdoor Area

Inspect your deck for:
- Soft or rotten timber boards
- Loose fixings or handrails
- Staining or algae growth

Clean with a water blaster, treat with decking oil or stain, and replace any damaged boards. If your deck needs significant repairs, get a [builder](/services/builder) to assess it.

### 6. ✅ Plumbing Check

Winter can hide slow leaks. In spring, check:
- Under sinks for signs of dripping
- Hot water cylinder for rust or sediment
- Outdoor taps (often damaged by frost in colder regions)
- Drainage around the house for standing water

Book a [licensed plumber](/services/plumbing) if you spot anything suspicious.

### 7. ✅ Electrical Safety Check

Test all smoke alarms — NZ law requires long-life photoelectric alarms. Also check:
- Outdoor lighting and power points for water damage
- RCDs (residual current devices) by pressing the test button on your switchboard

An [electrician](/services/electrical) can do a full safety inspection if your wiring is older.

### 8. ✅ Insulation Top-Up

Check your ceiling and underfloor insulation. The Healthy Homes Standards require landlords to have adequate insulation, but homeowners benefit too. Look for:
- Gaps or missing batts in the ceiling
- Damaged underfloor insulation (rodents, moisture)

[Insulation installers](/services/insulation) can assess and top up your insulation before winter returns.

### 9. ✅ Garden and Lawn

Spring is prime time for:
- Lawn aeration and fertilising
- Planting new garden beds
- Tree pruning before growth starts
- Fence repairs

A [landscaper](/services/landscaping-gardening) can handle the heavy lifting while you enjoy the season.

### 10. ✅ Window and Door Seals

Check weatherstripping around windows and doors. Drafty gaps increase heating costs significantly. This is a quick DIY job or a simple handyman task.

### 11. ✅ Pest Control

Warmer weather brings out pests — ants, wasps (especially German wasps in late summer), and rodents. If you saw any activity over winter, spring is the time to treat before populations boom. A [pest control professional](/services/pest-control) can lay bait stations and treat nests.

### 12. ✅ Exterior Cleaning

A water blaster makes a huge difference to:
- Driveways and paths (algae and mould)
- House exterior (moss on cladding)
- Fences and decks

Many cleaning companies and handymen offer water blasting as a spring special.

## Lawn Mowing Services in NZ: Prices, Frequency & Tips

A well-maintained lawn is one of the simplest ways to keep a property looking sharp. But between mowing, edging, and disposing of clippings, it takes more time than most people realise. Here's what lawn mowing services cost in New Zealand and how to find a reliable gardener.

## Lawn Mowing Prices in NZ

| Lawn Size | Typical Cost |
|---|---|
| Small (up to 100m²) | $40 – $70 |
| Medium (100–300m²) | $60 – $120 |
| Large (300–600m²) | $100 – $180 |
| Extra large (600m²+) | $150 – $300+ |

Prices typically include mowing, edging, and blowing down hard surfaces. Disposing of clippings (bagging or mulching) is usually included but worth confirming.

Most lawn mowing services charge based on time and/or lawn area. First visits are often priced higher as the lawn may need extra work to bring it to a maintained standard.

## How Often Should You Mow?

| Season | Frequency |
|---|---|
| Spring (Sep–Nov) | Every 1–2 weeks |
| Summer (Dec–Feb) | Every 1–2 weeks |
| Autumn (Mar–May) | Every 2–3 weeks |
| Winter (Jun–Aug) | Every 4–6 weeks (or as needed) |

In warmer northern regions like Auckland and Northland, lawns can grow year-round and may need fortnightly mowing even in winter.

## What's Usually Included (and What Costs Extra)

**Typically included:**
- Mowing (cylinder or rotary)
- Edge trimming along paths, driveways, and garden borders
- Blowing or raking clippings from hard surfaces

**Usually charged separately:**
- Garden bed weeding
- Hedge trimming
- Tree pruning
- Weed spraying
- Fertilising

If you want a comprehensive garden maintenance service, look for a gardener who offers a full-service package — many will combine mowing with regular weeding and seasonal tidy-ups for a fixed monthly fee.

## Finding a Reliable Lawn Mowing Service

**1. Check how long they've been operating** — established lawn services in your area know the local grass types and conditions.

**2. Ask about equipment** — professional-grade mowers (Honda, Husqvarna, Toro) give better results than domestic mowers.

**3. Confirm insurance** — public liability is important in case of property damage (broken windows from flying stones are surprisingly common).

**4. Look for consistency** — the best mowing services are reliable and show up at the same interval every time.

## What Is Airtasker?

Airtasker is an Australian on-demand marketplace that connects people with local workers for a wide range of tasks — from furniture assembly to cleaning. It launched in New Zealand several years ago but has never gained the same foothold it has across the Tasman.

## Why New Zealanders Are Looking for Alternatives

- **No licence verification** — Airtasker doesn't check whether workers hold the required NZ trade licences (e.g., registered plumbers, electricians)
- **No NZ-specific compliance** — NZ trade work has strict legal requirements under the Building Act, Electricity Act, and Plumbers, Gasfitters and Drainlayers Act
- **Inconsistent pricing** — Bidding models can lead to unrealistically low quotes followed by scope-creep disputes
- **Limited trade focus** — Airtasker works best for simple tasks, not skilled trade jobs
